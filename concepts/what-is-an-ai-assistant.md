# What is an AI Assistant?

**The short answer:** An AI assistant is a conversational AI tool you interact with through text (or sometimes voice). You give it instructions; it responds. The conversation can continue back and forth, building context as you go.

## The Tools You Are Using in This Curriculum

**Perplexity AI** (https://perplexity.ai)
Best for: Research, finding information with cited sources, quick factual answers. Perplexity searches the web in real time, which means its answers are more current than tools that rely solely on training data.

**Claude by Anthropic** (https://claude.ai)
Best for: Long documents, nuanced writing, careful reasoning, tasks that require following detailed instructions precisely. Claude tends to be thorough and thoughtful.

**ChatGPT by OpenAI** (https://chat.openai.com)
Best for: General tasks, brainstorming, coding assistance, wide variety of use cases. ChatGPT is the most widely known and has a large ecosystem of integrations.

## What They Have in Common

- All three are free to use at the basic level
- All three accept plain English instructions
- All three can be wrong - always verify important facts
- All three improve with more specific, detailed prompts
- None of them remember previous conversations by default (each new session starts fresh)

## The Most Important Thing to Know

These are tools, not oracles. They do not always know the right answer. They do not know your specific situation unless you tell them. The more context you provide, the more useful they become.
