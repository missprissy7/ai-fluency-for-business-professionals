# What is AI?

**The short answer:** AI (Artificial Intelligence) is software that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence - like understanding language, generating text, answering questions, and recognizing patterns.

## The Practical Definition

For your purposes as a business professional, AI is a tool you give instructions to in plain English and it gives you back useful work. You do not need to understand how it works internally any more than you need to understand how a car engine works to drive.

## What Makes Modern AI Different

The AI tools available today (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and others) are powered by Large Language Models - systems trained on enormous amounts of text that learned to understand and generate human language at a high level. What makes them remarkable is that you interact with them in plain English, just like talking to a knowledgeable colleague.

## What AI Is Good At

- Writing and rewriting text
- Summarizing long documents
- Answering questions
- Brainstorming and generating options
- Explaining complex topics simply
- Drafting emails, reports, and proposals
- Analyzing and organizing information

## What AI Is Not Good At

- Tasks requiring real-time data (it may not know what happened today)
- Tasks requiring personal relationships or trust
- Tasks where being wrong has serious consequences without your review
- Anything that requires a physical presence or action in the world

## The Key Thing to Remember

AI is a tool, not a replacement. You are still the professional. You set the direction, you evaluate the output, and you take responsibility for the final product. AI accelerates your work - it does not replace your judgment.
