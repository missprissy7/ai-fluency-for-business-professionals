# Prompt Template

Use this as your starting point for any AI task. Copy it, fill in the blanks, and refine until you get useful output.

---

## The Three-Part Structure

ROLE: You are a [type of expert or professional].

TASK: Your job is to [specific action] for [specific output].

FORMAT: Present your response as [structure, length, style].

---

## Add Context for Better Results

Audience: [Who will read or use this?]
Purpose: [Why does this need to exist?]
Situation: [Any relevant background?]
Constraints: [Length, tone, what to include, what to exclude]

---

## Filled-In Example

You are an experienced project manager who communicates clearly to non-technical audiences.

Your job is to write a project status update email for a client whose delivery has been delayed by 5 business days.

Audience: Director-level client, non-technical.
Purpose: Update them on the delay and reset expectations.
Constraints: Under 200 words. Direct tone. Include cause and new timeline. Exclude technical details about the bug.

Present as a professional email with subject line, greeting, body paragraphs, and a clear next step.

---

## Common ROLE Phrases

- You are an experienced [job title]...
- You are a communications expert specializing in [industry]...
- You are a senior consultant advising a [type of organization]...
- You are an editor reviewing work for a professional audience...
- You are a strategic advisor helping a business leader prepare for...

## Common FORMAT Instructions

- as a professional email with subject line
- as a bulleted list of no more than 8 items
- as a one-page memo with bold section headers
- as three short paragraphs each under 75 words
- as an executive summary of no more than 150 words
- as a numbered list of steps with a one-sentence explanation for each
